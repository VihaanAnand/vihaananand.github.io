How it works

8-week summer intensive

Each course runs for 8 weeks over the summer, meeting once a week. Sessions are focused and fast-paced, built for students who want to get ahead, review gaps in their math knowledge, or understand the material before the school year starts.

  • Online sessions via Google Meet, 1.5-2 hours each
  • Sessions cover concepts, practice problems, and Q&A
  • Weekly homework to practice concepts and track progress
  • $320 $300 total
